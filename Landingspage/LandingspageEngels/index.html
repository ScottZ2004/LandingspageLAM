What is the LAM?

The LAM is your museum in the Netherlands. Brand new and packed with international artwroks from the topshelf . Above all, the LAM is an innovative and personal museum that lets you see the artworks differently. For all ages, and we really mean all ages. With our personal approach you always get the best art experience.

Collection

An uninhibited look and discovering yourself. That's what the LAM is all about. That is why you will not find any sign with information about; who the artist is, the format and when the artwork was made.

Once you are captivated by the work and you want to go further into depth, we will of course be more than happy to tell you more about the artist's story. If you want any tips or challenges, we always have an employee nearby. Our colleagues are viewing coaches; they are happy to help them show the artwork in a special way.

Admire our artworks today!
